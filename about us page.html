About Us

My name is Ayan Patil, and I am the creator of the website, and I designed this platform to help people better understand the basics of personal finance in a simple and engaging way. The goal of this website is to make important financial topics easier to learn for the youth, especially for students who are just starting to explore economics. I focused on presenting clear explanations, structured sections, and interactive elements like quizzes to make learning more effective and enjoyable. Through this website, I hope users can build essential financial skills that will help them make smarter decisions, develop good habits, and feel more confident about managing money in the future.
